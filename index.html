About

Hilla Tea Estate Kali Mandir Line

Hamro Gaon(kali mandir line) is situated at Hilla Tea Estate Post Nagrakata Dist Jalapiguri West Bengal India. At the entry gate of village there is a temple(mandir) of goddess Maa Kali and god Shiva, So there is the reason behind the name of village Mandir Line. In these small village we have seventy houses out of which each and everyone belongs to Nepali Community. So each and everyonne lives with prosperity and brotherhood. From the height of our village, we can have a beautiful view of river Jaldhaka and the place like Bhutan, Bindhu, Jhalong, Darjeeling and many more
